Latest News Highlights

Egerton Researcher Secures Grant for Drought-Resistant Crops

Published: October 26, 2025

Dr. Mwangi from the Department of Agronomy and Crop Science has been awarded a competitive grant to study indigenous grains and develop new seed varieties capable of thriving in arid and semi-arid lands, boosting local food security.

Faculty Hosts National Symposium on Sustainable Dairy Farming

Published: October 15, 2025

The Faculty of Agriculture successfully hosted over 200 participants, including policymakers and farmers, for a three-day symposium focusing on zero-grazing techniques and climate-smart feeding practices in the Rift Valley region.

New Student Exchange Program with Wageningen University Announced

Published: September 30, 2025

Exciting opportunities await! The Dean's office confirms a partnership for student mobility, allowing final-year Agriculture students to participate in a semester-long research placement in the Netherlands starting next year.
