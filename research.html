Research Projects

Our students actively participate in research that addresses real-world agricultural challenges.

  • Climate-Resilient Crops Initiative
  • Smart Irrigation Systems
  • Organic Fertilizer Development
  • AI for Pest Control

Climate-Resilient Maize Trials

Lead: Dr. Achieng • Location: Nakuru

Testing new maize varieties that can withstand irregular rainfall and extended dry spells.

Solar-Powered Smart Irrigation

Lead: Eng. Kiptoo • Location: University Farm

Combining solar panels and soil moisture sensing to reduce water wastage in smallholder farms.

Organic Fertilizer from Waste

Lead: Ms. Wanjiru • Location: Bioconversion Lab

Converting market waste into safe, nutrient-rich organic fertilizer for local farmers.

AI for Pest Detection

Lead: CS & Agriculture Students • Location: Digital Lab

Using computer vision to detect pest damage on leaves and alert farmers through a mobile app.

Dairy Data Analytics

Lead: Dairy Unit • Location: Campus Dairy Farm

Tracking milk yields and feed efficiency using sensors and dashboards to guide farm decisions.

Soil Health Mapping

Lead: Soil Science Dept. • Location: County Farms

Students collect soil samples and use GIS to map nutrient levels across the region.
